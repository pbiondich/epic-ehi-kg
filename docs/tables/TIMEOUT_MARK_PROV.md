# TIMEOUT_MARK_PROV

> This table contains the provider(s) who marked the surgical site on the patient prior to surgery.

**Primary key:** `RECORD_ID`, `CONTACT_DATE_REAL`, `LINE`  
**Columns:** 5

[← index](../index.md)

| 1 | `RECORD_ID` | NUMERIC | PK shared | The unique identifier for the timeout record. |
| 2 | `CONTACT_DATE_REAL` | FLOAT | PK | A unique contact date in decimal format. The integer portion of the number indicates the date of contact.
